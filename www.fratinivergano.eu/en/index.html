Practice areas

FratiniVergano specialises in European and International Law, notably EU competition and internal market law, WTO and EU trade law, EU agricultural and food law, EU regulation and public affairs.

The firm has an important industry focus, its lawyers having significant experience in the sectors of electronic communications, postal services, regulated professions, gambling, agriculture and food law.

The languages of the firm are English and Italian; we also correspond in French, Spanish and German.

FratiniVergano has a EU team in Brussels and a ASEAN team in Singapore
