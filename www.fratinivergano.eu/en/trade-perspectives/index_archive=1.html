With this fortnightly publication, we intend to regularly provide readers with a set of recent international trade developments of particular commercial and regulatory interest. Our focus will be on those matters that are set to become contentious trade issues or which should be followed closely by traders, government officials, lawyers, consultants and all other concerned parties. Each issue of Trade Perspectives© includes at least one article related to food law or food safety. A dedicated index of these articles can be found here: Trade Perspectives: Index of Food Law and Safety Articles.

We hope that you will find our new publication interesting and stimulating.  We look forward to hearing from you with your comments, questions, arguments and/or conflicting views.  FratiniVergano trusts that the debate that may result from Trade Perspectives© will represent our modest contribution to enhancing the interpretation of international trade law and to creating greater awareness among businesses and civil society.

Trade Perspectives© is issued with the purpose of informing on new developments in international trade and stimulating reflections on the legal and commercial issues involved.  Trade Perspectives© does not constitute legal advice and is not, therefore, intended to be relied on or create any client/lawyer relationship.
